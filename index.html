About

This game aims to maximize the potential of every person. Everyone, especially girls, doubts their ability to succeed. This phenomenon, often referred to as imposter syndrome, is often one of the biggest obstacles a person can face, especially in their career. By recognizing your own negative thoughts as false, you can disregard them and focus on the fact that you are a skilled, competent individual.
